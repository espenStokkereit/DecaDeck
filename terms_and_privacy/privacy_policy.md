# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stokkereit Technologies collects, stores, and uses data related to the mobile application “Deca Deck”.

Please also read our [Terms and Conditions](terms_and_conditions.md).

## Definition of Terms

In this notice, “we”, “us”, and “our” refer to Stokkereit Technologies. The “Application” refers to the mobile application “Deca Deck”, and the “Service” refers to the Application and any related services we control.

In this text, “user” means you as an individual using the Service. “Data” refers to information that may relate to the user.

## Overview

Deca Deck does not collect, transmit, store, or share personal data with us.

All user data created and used by the Application is stored locally on the device on which the Application is installed. We do not have access to that data.

## Data Collection

Deca Deck does not collect personal data from users.

The Application may store certain app-related information locally on your device in order to function properly. This may include, for example:

- flashcard decks
- progress and study history
- app preferences and settings
- other content created or edited by the user within the app

This information is stored only on your device and is not sent to us.

## Data Use

Because we do not collect or receive your data, we do not use your personal data for analytics, marketing, profiling, or any other purpose.

Locally stored app data is used only to provide the Application’s functionality on your device.

## Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties because we do not collect it.

## Service Providers

Deca Deck does not use third-party services for analytics, advertising, account management, or cloud storage in a way that sends us your personal data.

If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

## Cookies

Deca Deck does not use cookies.

## Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store your data on our servers, we do not retain personal data.

All app data remains on your device until you delete it or remove the Application, subject to how your device and operating system manage local storage and backups.

## Data Security

Because Deca Deck stores data locally on your device, the security of that data depends in part on the security of your device and operating system.

We do not transmit or store your personal data on our own servers.

## Data Access and Deletion

You remain in control of the data stored locally by the Application.

You may delete your data by removing content within the app or by uninstalling the Application from your device, depending on how the operating system handles local app data.

## Children

Deca Deck does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Because the Application does not collect personal data, there is no intentional collection of personally identifiable information from children under 13.

If you believe otherwise, please contact us.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated “Last updated” date.
